# Kino Urla Restaurant Website

A modern, responsive website for Kino Urla restaurant and bar located in Urla, İzmir, Turkey.

## Project Overview

Kino Urla is a comprehensive restaurant website showcasing a full digital menu system for a contemporary dining establishment. The website presents breakfast, lunch, dinner, drinks, and cocktail offerings in an elegant, easy-to-navigate format.

## Features

### Design Elements
- Clean, minimalist layout with authentic restaurant aesthetics
- Fully responsive design optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- Professional typography matching the brand identity
- Color scheme consistent with original branding materials
- Smooth scrolling navigation between menu sections

### Content Sections
1. **Header Section**: Restaurant branding with logo and contact information
2. **Breakfast Menu (Kahvaltı)**: Traditional Turkish breakfast offerings including serpme kahvaltı and quick breakfast options
3. **Appetizers (Başlangıçlar)**: Cheese plates and charcuterie selections
4. **Salads (Salatalar)**: Fresh salad varieties including Caesar, arugula parmesan, and grilled halloumi
5. **Pizza**: Extensive pizza menu with Italian and Turkish fusion options
6. **Pasta (Makarna)**: Classic Italian pasta dishes with various sauces
7. **Main Courses (Ana Yemekler)**: Grilled meats and pan-cooked dishes
8. **Burgers**: Gourmet burger selection with premium toppings
9. **Snacks (Atıştırmalıklar)**: Fries, toasts, wraps, tacos, and nachos
10. **Kino Crispy World**: Fried chicken specialties and sharing platters
11. **Hot Beverages (Sıcak İçecekler)**: Coffee varieties and Turkish tea
12. **Cold Beverages (Soğuk İçecekler)**: Soft drinks, fresh juices, and specialty drinks
13. **Desserts (Tatlılar)**: Cheesecakes, brownies, and specialty desserts
14. **Draft Beers (Fıçı Biralar)**: Selection of draft beers
15. **Bottled Beers (Şişe Biralar)**: Domestic and imported bottled beers
16. **Set Menus**: Combination deals with beer and food
17. **Cocktails**: Classic and signature cocktail menu
18. **Wines**: Red, white, and rosé wine selections
19. **Campaign Section**: Special offers and promotions

### Interactive Features
- Direct links to Instagram (@kinourla)
